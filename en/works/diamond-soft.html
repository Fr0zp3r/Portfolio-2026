Digital look & feel.

Defining how the brand should read digitally before producing pieces — colour, type and air built for B2B.
Focus
Bring visual criterion to the brand at its digital layer — not redesign the full system. Coherence between pieces without claiming a global rebrand.
Visual leadership
Creative decisions to sustain consistency across pieces. Look & feel defined at the deliverable level, not at foundational system level.

Pieces under the system.

Digital deliverables aligned to the defined system — coherence piece by piece, without overload.

It isn't always about redesigning — sometimes what's missing is criterion that holds up what already exists.
