The character leads.

Before the logo or the colour, the character. A simple, reproducible icon that can carry the brand on its own.
Premise
Character first. Typography and colour picked to support it — not to compete with it.
Reproduction
Built to print cheap — bags, t-shirts, boxes — and stay recognisable at first glance.

A system around the icon.
