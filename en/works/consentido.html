The rules of the system.

Logo, palette, typography and composition — the four pillars that define how the brand reads before any application.
System pillars
Logo · five-tone palette · typographic hierarchy · editorial composition — the base for every application.
Criterion
Hold consistency without overplaying the system. The brand had to read the same across every touchpoint.

Adapted to verticals.

The applications keep the system's editorial hierarchy: square posts, verticals and carousels prepared per channel, not cropped by inertia.

The challenge wasn't to redesign the brand — it was to make sure every application held the same criterion.
